1. What is the difference between `var` and `let`?
    
You can reassign and redeclare with `var`, and it can be accessed outside of a code block. However, you cannot redeclare a `let` variable.
    
2. What is the difference between `var` and `const`?
    
You can reassign and redeclare with `var`, and it can be accessed outside of a code block. However, you cannot reassign nor redeclare a `const` variable.
    
3. What is the difference between `let` and `const`?
    
You can reassign with the `let` keyword, but not with the `const` keyword.
    
4. What is hoisting?
    
When using the `var` keyword, the variables' declaration are “hoisted” to the top of the scope.
